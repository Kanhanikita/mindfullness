Tips to overcome Anger

  • Slowly repeat a calm word or phrase such as “relax,” “take it easy.” Repeat it to yourself while breathing deeply.
  • Use exercise and sports as a way to release anger and as a way to redirect energy
  • Keep track of situations, people, or thoughts that often trigger your anger. Knowing your triggers can help you prepare for and handle them better.
  • Journaling can be a great way to release your emotions. Write about what made you angry and how you feel about it.
