Mission

"We strive to help students deal with stress, by providing a space where they can journal, keep a calandar, and learn study and destressing techniques."

Here we have compiled a list of tricks and tips that can help you maintain your mental well-being, from your basic needs to managing academic stress. We have also included a few of our favourite study methods as well!

Basic Needs

Eating Well: Eating

Staying Active: Physical activity

Being Social: Socailising is incredibly important to maintaining good mental wellbeing, especially in a post-Covid era. Being social allows you to make connections which in turn makgives you a sense of belonging. Being around others gives you a chance to recieve emotional support and it also allows you to help others who need it.

Academic Stress

This is something that you are going to deal with alot throughout your time at post-secondary, so it's best that you know how to effectively manage it.

Plan Ahead: Knowing when a test is or when an assignment is due can save you a lot of trouble! Putting all of the dates of tests and when assignments are due can give you a good overview of what needs to be done first.

Don't fall Behind: Falling behind in a class is something that you do want to happen during post-secondary. Falling behind makes it difficult to understand what is happening during the current lecture as well as the future lectures. Becuase they were designed with the idea that you already understand the ideas that were taught in the previous lectures. You can get help by reaching out to your instructor to go over a concept that you find difficult, but it is important to understand these topics so that you can understand ones in the future.

Speak to someone

Some post-secondary institutions provide consuling for their students, this is a great place for you to talk about how you fell and to get help while on campus. If what you are dealing with is an academic matter, ask your instructors for help and clarification or go to peer tutoring sessions.
