Tips to overcome Stress

  • Plan your day with a balanced schedule that includes time for classes, studying, breaks, and relaxation.
  • Take a few moments to breathe deeply. Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, and exhale for 4 seconds. This can help calm your nervous system.
  • Pay attention to the present moment without judgment. Take a quick 10-minute walk outside. It can help clear your mind and improve your mood.
  • It’s okay to say no to additional tasks or activities if you’re already feeling overwhelmed. Prioritize your well-being and focus on what’s most important.
  • Work for 25-30 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. This can help prevent burnout and keep you focused.
  • Listening to your favorite music can help reduce stress and improve your mood. Calming genres like classical, lo-fi, or nature sounds can be especially effective.
