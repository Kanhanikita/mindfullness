Tips to overcome Sadness

  • t’s okay to feel sad. Instead of ignoring your emotions, acknowledge them. This is the first step towards healing.
  • Moving your body like walking or running, working out, dancing, or playing sports can help uplift the mood.
  • Spending time in nature has been shown to reduce feelings of sadness. Go for a walk in a park or sit outside to get some fresh air.
  • Reduce negative self talks. Replace negative thoughts like “I’m not good enough” with affirmations such as “I am doing my best, and that’s okay.”
  • Share your feelings with a friend, family member, or mentor. Talking it out can provide relief and help you feel less isolated.
