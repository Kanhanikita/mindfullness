Tips to overcome Procastination

  • Large tasks can feel overwhelming and lead to procrastination. Break them down into smaller, manageable pieces to make them less intimidating.
  • It’s good to relax, but set a timer for 10-15 minutes. Once it goes off, get back to work.
  • If a task can be completed in 2 minutes or less, do it immediately. This helps you avoid the temptation of putting it off and losing momentum.
  • Allocate specific blocks of time for different tasks and activities. This prevents excessive relaxing and helps you stay focused.
  • Study Groups can help keep you on track and create a sense of responsibility to get work done.
  • Set a clear intention for your breaks (e.g., “I’ll stretch and drink water”) rather than defaulting to mindless activities.
